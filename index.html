GNSS Antenna

As part of my Electrical Engineering capstone project, I designed and analysed a compact dual-band GNSS antenna system operating at the L1 and L5 frequencies for autonomous vehicle applications. The project involved RF and antenna design, ADS simulation, S-parameter and noise figure analysis, and PCB layout, with a strong focus on system-level performance and integration. This work strengthened my understanding of RF front-end design, antenna optimisation, and real-world constraints in high-frequency systems.

2026P3

PLC Control System for an Automated Packaging Quality Assurance

As part of my fourth-year robotics and manufacturing automation course, my team and I built the PLC control system for the final quality assurance station of an automated nut-and-bolt packaging line. The project involved ladder logic design in RSLogix 500, OPC tag integration with NI Vision Builder, scan cycle and latency planning, and operator interface design through PLC-driven indicator lights. This work strengthened my understanding of industrial control architecture, deterministic scan-cycle behaviour, and how clean sensor interfaces shape every downstream control decision.
